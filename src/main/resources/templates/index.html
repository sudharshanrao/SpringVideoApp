Video Stream over HTTP App

This sample application streams S3 video content over HTTP using the Amazon S3 Java V2 API.

  1. Upload MP4 videos to an Amazon S3 bucket by choosing the Upload Video.
  2. Fill in the form and then choose Submit.
  3. The application stores the video in an Amazon S3 bucket.
  4. You can view a video by choosing Watch Video.
  5. Next, choose the video to watch by click in the video title.
